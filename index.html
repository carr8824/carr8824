Ph.D. in Economics and Business from Universitat Jaume I, Castellón, Spain. I successfully defended my thesis on July 26, 2024, earning the summa cum laude distinction. Specializing in asset management, pricing strategies, and predictive analytics, I integrate deep financial expertise with advanced data science and machine learning techniques.

My dissertation, “Decision-Making under Flow Dynamics: Deconstructing Behavioral Patterns in Portfolio Management” (View Dissertation), bridges empirical asset pricing with causal inference, providing critical insights into financial stability and investor behavior.
