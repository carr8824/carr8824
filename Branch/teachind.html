Research Methods and Techniques This course is designed to equip students with the knowledge and skills required to conduct effective research and write insightful research papers. The course will cover essential facts to consider before starting a research project, as well as dos and don'ts when beginning a research endeavor. Students will also learn how to generate research ideas using creativity, attitude, and technicity. The course will provide guidance on becoming an experienced researcher and conducting in-depth research. Additionally, students will learn how to choose and develop a research topic and craft a catchy research question. The course will also cover the effective literature review process, including search procedures and source structures, and the use of reference managers to organize research materials. Overall, this course aims to provide students with a practical and comprehensive understanding of research methods and techniques that will enable them to conduct effective research and produce high-quality research papers.
