Cristhian Rodriguez-Revilla

Biography

Cristhian Andres Rodriguez Revilla is a passionate finance researcher and Ph.D. candidate with a track record of academic excellence and professional accomplishments. Born in Bucaramanga, Colombia, Cristhian has pursued his passion for economics, finance, and data science, earning degrees from prestigious institutions such as Universidad de Antioquia, Universidad del Rosario, and Universidad Carlos III de Madrid.

Cristhian graduated among the top students from the prestigious Universidad de Antioquia, where he earned his Bachelor's degree in Economics. His academic excellence in financial markets, investments, and asset management did not go unnoticed.

During his Master's in Quantitative Finance at the renowned Universidad del Rosario, Cristhian received a scholarship and was part of the Jovenes Investigadores research scholar program. His exceptional thesis on predictive models of interest rates not only earned him the Summa Cum Laude distinction but also the national award for the most outstanding dissertation in economics with an emphasis on finance, organized by AsoBancaria. Furthermore, he had the opportunity to publish a summary of his thesis in B&E magazine, a publication for practitioners.

Cristhian continued his academic pursuits in Madrid, Spain, earning a Master's in Business and Finance at Universidad Carlos III de Madrid. He maintained a top-tier GPA, leading to a Predoc Scholarship from the UC3M Department of Business and Finance.

In his second year as a Ph.D. candidate, Cristhian secured a grant from the Spanish Ministry of Science to continue his research at Universidad Jaume I (UJI) and support a project analyzing the decisions of individuals in strategic environments. This grant provided financial resources for three years and allowed him to visit J. Mack Robinson College of Business at Georgia State University for three months, where he attended classes, meetings, and seminars from the CEAR group.

Cristhian's extensive experience in research, data science, and predictive models has equipped him with the skills and expertise to provide valuable solutions to businesses and individuals. With a strong sense of commitment and ethics, he is open to exploring new opportunities for collaboration and success.

Interview with Cristhian Andres Rodriguez Revilla

Journalist: Cristhian, thank you for joining us today. Your journey in the world of academia and finance is inspiring. What motivated you to pursue a career in finance and data science?

Cristhian: Thank you for having me. I've always been passionate about math and had a natural curiosity for computers. When I pursued my Bachelor's in economics, I became interested in financial markets, investments, and asset management. My Master's in Quantitative Finance and subsequent studies in big data analytics further fueled my passion for data science and finance.

Journalist: You've faced some challenging circumstances, including living in a conflict-ridden area and dealing with the Covid-19 pandemic. How have these experiences shaped you as a person and professional?

Cristhian: These experiences have taught me the importance of perseverance, resilience, and faith. My family and I found solace and strength in Jesus Christ amidst turmoil, and I've carried that strength throughout my journey. During the pandemic, I learned to adapt and focus on my mental health and personal growth, which has made me a more well-rounded individual.

Journalist: You've achieved significant academic success, including awards and publications. What has been the key to your accomplishments?

Cristhian: I believe that talent and effort go hand in hand. I've dedicated myself to my studies and research, always striving to learn and grow. I also recognize the importance of humility and acknowledging my weaknesses, which has allowed me to continually improve.

Journalist: Your faith and values have played a significant role in shaping your life and career. How do they influence your professional aspirations and your approach to connecting with others in your field?

Cristhian: My faith and values have instilled in me a strong sense of purpose and a desire to make a positive impact on others. In my professional life, I prioritize creating an environment of trust, loyalty, discipline, and kindness. This approach not only helps me connect with others on a deeper level but also enables me to foster a collaborative and supportive network that shares my commitment to innovation and excellence.

Journalist: As you look towards the future, what are your aspirations for your career and personal growth?

Cristhian: As I look to the future, I aspire to transition from academia to the industry, leveraging my knowledge, creativity, and innovation to make a meaningful impact in finance. I am passionate about developing applications and startups that simplify processes and foster collaboration among professionals, researchers, and companies.

My goal is to forge valuable connections with renowned managers, CEOs, and researchers worldwide, and showcase my potential to recruiters and companies. I am eager to bring a fresh perspective and innovative solutions to the industry, despite my primarily academic background.

I envision creating cutting-edge solutions using data science, machine learning, and artificial intelligence to enhance decision-making and drive growth. I am committed to continuous personal and professional growth, embracing challenges and new experiences to become a more resourceful and impactful individual in the finance industry.
