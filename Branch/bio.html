Cristhian Rodriguez-Revilla, PhD

Biography

Cristhian Andres Rodriguez Revilla is a financial economist and data scientist with a proven track record of academic excellence and professional achievements. Born in Bucaramanga, Colombia, he has dedicated his career to the intersection of economics, finance, and technology.

Graduating among the top of his class from Universidad de Antioquia with a Bachelor's degree in Economics, Cristhian's exceptional understanding of financial markets, investments, and asset management set the foundation for his future endeavors.

Pursuing his passion further, he obtained a Master's in Quantitative Finance from Universidad del Rosario. Awarded a scholarship and selected for the JÃ³venes Investigadores research program, he produced a groundbreaking thesis on predictive models of interest rates. This work earned him the Summa Cum Laude distinction and the national award for the most outstanding dissertation in economics with an emphasis on finance by AsoBancaria. A summary of his thesis was also published in B&E magazine, reaching a wide audience of industry professionals.

Continuing his academic journey in Spain, Cristhian completed a Master's in Business and Finance at Universidad Carlos III de Madrid, maintaining a top-tier GPA. His academic excellence earned him a Predoc Scholarship from the UC3M Department of Business and Finance.

In his second year as a Ph.D. candidate, he received a grant from the Spanish Ministry of Science to further his research at Universidad Jaume I (UJI), focusing on strategic decision-making. This opportunity included a three-month visit to the J. Mack Robinson College of Business at Georgia State University, where he engaged with the CEAR group through classes, meetings, and seminars.

Upon successfully defending his Ph.D. thesis, Cristhian was awarded the Summa Cum Laude distinction for his outstanding research contributions.

Professionally, Cristhian has recently collaborated with a European consulting firm specializing in business development. Over the past month, he has worked with banks and insurance companies to enhance their business models at the commercial level, facilitating digital transformation. His role involves mentoring and guiding internal teams in adopting leadership strategies, implementing new business policies, and leveraging cutting-edge technologies.

With a strong commitment to ethical practices and a passion for innovation, Cristhian is open to exploring new collaborative opportunities that drive success and make a meaningful impact.
